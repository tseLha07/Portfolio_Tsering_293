About Me

Meet Choekyi Anodunkhartsang, a vibrant Tibetan woman with a passion for creativity and craftsmanship. At the age of 40, Choekyi has mastered the art of crochet, skillfully weaving colorful yarns into intricate designs. Her nimble fingers dance across the hooks, bringing life to her imaginative creations. Born and raised in the picturesque valleys of Tibet, Choekyi's love for crocheting is deeply rooted in her cultural heritage, where handcrafted textiles are treasured as symbols of beauty and tradition. With each stitch, she weaves together stories of resilience, patience, and the enduring spirit of her people. Choekyi's crochet journey not only reflects her artistic prowess but also serves as a testament to her unwavering dedication and unwavering love for preserving the rich tapestry of her Tibetan identity.
